Clash Royale is an online multiplayer card battle arena game. Play competative 1v1 or some chill 2v2! Put together a deck of eight cards from over sixty unique cards, ranging from devastating spells such as a fireball, a troop such as the handsome knight, or a spawner such as the party house itself, the Goblin Hut. Combine different cards to achieve devastating combos and destroy your opponent. Play special gamemodes such as draft, where you pick a card and give your opponent a card! Crush your foes and gain trophies to progress through the arenas and unlock cards. Join tournaments and rise to the top of the leaderboard and beat your friends. Constant balance changes are released that shift the meta, so you never get bored of the game by simply playing one deck over and over. Check out Clash Royale here! Keep Calm and Clash On!
