Hearthstone is a cartoonish card game set in the world of World of Warcraft. In Hearthstone, you build decks of thirty cards and use the deck to wreck your opponents. You can pick between nine classes, each with different cards and abilities, to build your deck. Of course, you can also build multiple decks at a time! Enter different game modes, ranging from Standard to an Arena Battle, where you build a deck from scratch. You can also do solo adventures if everyone else sucks too much. Come check it out here!
