About

Explanations!

Vainglory is a F2P mobile MOBA first developed by Super Evil Mega Corp. Starting from Singapore, SEMC utilizes the spectacular E.V.I.L. Engine for its graphics. Choose from 30+ unique heroes, each with their own unique lore, abilities, and skins, to play in epic 3v3 battles on the Halcyon Fold. Play causual mode, which includes one lane and a jungle. Or, play Battle Royale, where your heroes are randomly selected and the jungle is closed off if you want some quick action. Destroy the enemy Vain Crystal to win by pushing through their turrets to win. Furthermore, if you want some intense five minute action, play blitz, where the first team to fifteen kills wins! Join guilds of 50 players and team up with your freinds in 6 player teams to dominate the leaderboards. For more information, visit their website here! Party up and play today!
