Explanations!

Vainglory is a F2P mobile MOBA first developed by Super Evil Mega Corp. Choose from 30+ heroes in epic 3v3 battles. There are three gamemodes, casual, battle royale, and blitz. Causal is your standard MOBA, where the objective is to destroy the enemy's vain crystal by defeating all their turrets whilst defending your own. In battle royale, the objective is the same, however, the jungle is blocked off, your hero is randomly chosen, and talents are enabled! Talents are special level-able perks that you can give to a hero. Blitz is a 3v3 deathmatch, where the first to 15 points wins. Each kill is one point, while all major objectives are 3 points. Join guilds of 50 players and team up with your freinds in 6 player teams. For more information, visit their website here! Party up and play today!
